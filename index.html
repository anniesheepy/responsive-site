Dine in Capilano Heights


Serving the neighborhood for over 45 years, Capilano heights is now run by the 3rd generation of the Sun family. Keeping the local favourite western Chinese style food and bringing some new twist from authentic Taiwanese food. Be prepared to rock your tastebuds.

Cook with the Drunken Monkey


At cooking with the Drunken Monkey you will have the opportunity to learn how to cook authentic Chinese food in a fully equipped professional kitchen. We offer classes for students with any level of experience, from complete beginners to seasoned veterans.
